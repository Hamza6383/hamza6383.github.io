TECHNICAL

I have also had the opportunity to work on various technical and building regulations packs throughout these 2 years of working proffesionally. Techinal or rather Building Regulations packs are essential documents that provide detailed information and guidance on the technical and regulatory requirements for a building project. My experience includes working on packs for both commercial and residential projects, ensuring that all necessary regulations are met and the process of constructing the building runs smoothly. In addition, I have also closely collaborated with Structural Engineers, and other consultants to ensure that the design and construction process adheres to local and national building codes. My expertise in this area has allowed me to produce high-quality and detailed Technical/Buiding Regulation packs that meet the needs of clients while also complying with all relevant regulations and standards.
