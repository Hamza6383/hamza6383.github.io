WELCOME TO MY PORTFOLIO WEBSITE

Welcome to my architecture portfolio website. Here, I will showcase my technical, design and other skills as an architectural Technologist. I aim to demonstrate my expertise through diverse projects that highlight creativity and attention to detail. The portfolio features residential and commercial designs, my design process, sustainability approach, and experience working with clients.

I created this portfolio website from scratch using CSS, HTML, and some JavaScript that I learned over the past few months. I customized the design, including fonts, colors, and layout, and added a parallax effect to the header to create depth and movement. This website showcases my creativity, attention to detail, and expertise as an architectural technologist.
