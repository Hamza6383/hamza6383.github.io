Welcome to my portfolio website. I am a passionate and driven Architectural Technologist with over 4 years of post-graduate experience, currently completing a Master’s in Façade Engineering, with only my dissertation remaining, due for submission in April 2026. I aim to be at the forefront of design innovation, contributing to a more sustainable, zero-carbon future for the built environment.

I have a deep passion for all aspects of design, from conceptual thinking to detailed technical resolution. I love creating designs that are not only imaginative and expressive but also logical, buildable, and performance-driven. My fascination with how elements come together to form cohesive, functional, and inspiring architecture led me to pursue façade engineering, where I continue to refine my understanding of materials, performance, and sustainability.

With a strong interest in BIM and emerging digital design technologies, I enjoy exploring new tools and methods that improve efficiency, coordination, and creativity within the design process. Skilled in communicating ideas through various mediums, from hand sketches and physical models to advanced 3D and digital platforms, I’m continually motivated to learn, adapt, and refine my craft through curiosity and innovation.

I created this portfolio website from scratch using CSS, HTML, and some JavaScript that I learned over the past few months. I customized the design, including fonts, colors, and layout, and added a parallax effect to the header to create depth and movement. This website showcases my creativity, attention to detail, and expertise as an architectural technologist.
